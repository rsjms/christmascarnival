F.A.Q.

Contact us if you have a different question

Number of kids expected to come and whether each kid can play the game once or any number of times?

Coupons will be given as it will be more resourceful and controlled , number of coupons will be mentioned later.

Can we include members later as well?

Yes we will be communicating a final deadline for you to make any changes in your team. Post that for security reasons as passes will be issued , changes in team wouldn’t be allowed.

Stall installation will be done or not?

Tall basic structure will be provided along with 2 tables , chairs and cloth. That is a standard for all teams. Decoration of the stall is entirely upto the team.

Can we include entertaining games like train or bumping car or pottery?

You can share your suggestions. The school will be deciding and approving the final games.

Who is going to decorate the stalls?

Decorations is responsibility of the team. They can encourage kids in the class to contribute buntings. Sharing a video sample of how parents have seemed support from entire parent community for the same.

How many games can be kept per stall?

Give as many ideas you can. We will sort out all the forms and get back to you. Considering the size of the stall, not more than 2 games at a game stall, else it can become chaotic.

Dimensions of the stall?

  • 8ft x 10ft for games/Activity stall
  • 8ft x 15ft for Food Stall

Game/Activity/Food Stall charges

no stall charges will be taken. It's a RSJMS community event, and the intent is to come together, eat together and kids to enjoy their own carnival. Games or food would NOT be charged. No monetary exchange of any sort in the carnival.

Number of volunteer parents for any stall?

Number of parents can be minimum 3 but the upper limit can be discussed , since there are two slots of the carnival. Try to include more and more parents but in presentable numbers. .

Only 1 member out of the group needs to register Or everyone has to register?

Only one member can fill in the form and share details of all participants.

Due to some exigency, a parent needs to back out from the group Can she do it and other members can take the responsibility?

Yes, you all can manage but this all has to be sorted within a timeline which will beinformed to you. Post the final date no more changes can happen. Once the IDs are given , someone can back out but cannot replace.

Can we include members later as well?

yes we can add more members but not after a final dead line is communicated

Who is going to decorate the stalls?

Parents and kids are responsible for decoration of their stalls.

Do we have mention all the kids name involved in a stall?

Children name for first three are mandatory, but after that it's not mandatory.
